Water

The surface of the sea

Sarah Cassidy has explored painting the surface of water for over 10 years. Each painting is titled chronologically.

Water is a symbol of the unconscious and has the ability to recall vivid memories of place. Through each painting, Cassidy explores her relationship with water and what its reflections—physical and metaphorical—have to teach us about our own perspective and perceptions.
