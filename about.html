About the artist

Your Name

Sarah Cassidy has explored painting natural subjects such as the surface of water for over 10 years. Her intense concentration on the surface of water, molecular particles floating through the air, and the intricacies of tree cross sections fascinate her and always provide an opportunity to go deeper.

Through each painting, Cassidy explores her relationship with the natural environment and what its reflections and patterns—physical and metaphorical—have to teach us about our own minds.

Sarah is based in Brooklyn, NY and works out of Brooklyn Art Cluster in Gowanus.
